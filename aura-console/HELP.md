# Aura Console In-App Help

## Getting Started
- Use the sidebar to navigate between tools, dashboard, and settings.
- Connect your Shopify store or other platform in Project Setup.
- Use the Dashboard for a quick overview of your store’s health and automation status.

## Tools Overview
- **Automation Scheduler:** Schedule and manage automations for your store.
- **Content Health Auditor:** Audit and improve your store’s SEO and content quality.
- **Fix Queue:** Review and resolve issues detected by automation engines.
- **Draft Library:** Manage and edit content drafts for your projects.
- **Products List:** View and optimize your Shopify products for SEO.
- **Reports:** Get insights and analytics on your store’s performance.
- **Credits:** View and purchase credits for running automations and AI features.

## Usage Tips
- Hover icons and cards for tooltips and extra info.
- Use the “Fix Queue” to address urgent issues.
- Export audit results and reports for offline review.
- Use the search and filters in each tool to quickly find what you need.
- Check the Changelog for new features and improvements.

## Error Handling
- Errors are shown as toast notifications or inline messages.
- If something goes wrong, check your internet connection and API status.
- For persistent issues, contact support or check the README for troubleshooting.

## Advanced
- All tools are modular and can be extended in `src/tools/`.
- Automated tests cover all major features—run `npm test` for diagnostics.
- For developers: see the root README.md for setup, testing, and deployment instructions.
