# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Aura Core Monolith collects, uses, and protects your information.

## What We Collect
- Email addresses and user account data (encrypted at rest)
- Project and content data you create or upload
- Analytics and usage data (for product improvement)

## How We Use Data
- To provide and improve the service
- For authentication and security
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Protection
- All sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit
- Access is restricted to authorized users only
- Audit logging is enabled for critical actions

## Data Export & Deletion
- You may request a copy of your data or deletion at any time by contacting support
- Data export is available via the API or by request

## Third-Party Services
- OAuth/SSO providers (e.g., Google) are used for authentication only
- No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing

## Contact
For privacy questions or requests, contact the project maintainers.
